Wizard Tag

Duel against your friends with virtual magic spells and your voice

Wizard tag is a fork of Lasertag created for Purdue University's new student orientation. Much of the technology in this game, such as the live envrionment scanning and lighting effects, comes from that project.

Players yell one of three spells − 'fireball', 'lightning', or 'shield' − and compete to score the most elimintations against the opposing team.

I use Vosk ➔, an on-device speech recognition model, to detect magic spell commands. While Vosk isn't as responsive as a bespoke wake-word model might be, latency is still low enough for spells to feel suitably responsive.
