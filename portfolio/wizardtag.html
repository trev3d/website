Wizard Tag

Duel against your friends with virtual magic spells and your voice

A fork of Lasertag created for Purdue University's new student orientation. Much of the technology in this game, such as the live envrionment scanning and lighting effects, comes from the lasertag project.

Players yell one of three spells ('fireball', 'lightning', or 'shield') and compete to score the most elimintations against their opposing team. I use Vosk ➔, an on-device speech recognition model/library, to detect spoken spells. While Vosk isn't as responsive as a bespoke 'wake-work' model might be, latency is still low enough for the game to feel suitably responsive.

Purdue University's Fusion Studio ➔ funded this project with a grant as part of their new student orientation Entertainment Challenge ➔
